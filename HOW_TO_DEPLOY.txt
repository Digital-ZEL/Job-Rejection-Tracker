FASTEST WAY (5 MINUTES):
------------------------

1. Go to this website: https://app.netlify.com/drop

2. Sign up for a free account (or login if you have one)

3. Drag your ENTIRE project folder onto the page
   (The folder: /Users/dennyt8/Job-Rejection-Tracker)

4. Wait 30 seconds

5. You'll get a live URL like: https://your-app-name.netlify.app

6. DONE! Your app is now live on the internet!


ALTERNATIVE: Using Vercel (Also Free)
--------------------------------------

1. Install Vercel: Open Terminal and type:
   npm install -g vercel

2. Go to your project folder:
   cd /Users/dennyt8/Job-Rejection-Tracker

3. Deploy:
   vercel

4. Follow the prompts

5. Done! You get a live URL


WHAT YOU NEED:
--------------
- Your project folder (you have it!)
- A free account on Netlify or Vercel
- That's it!


COST:

FREE! Both Netlify and Vercel have free tiers that are perfect for your app.

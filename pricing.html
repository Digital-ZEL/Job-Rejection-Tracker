Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Sign up for the Professional plan and get full access to all premium features for 14 days. No credit card required. After 14 days, you'll be charged $14.99/month. Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains yours. If you cancel, you can export all your data before your subscription ends. Free users can access basic features with some limitations.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never store your credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed through secure payment providers.
